kya studies how AI agents shop: what they see, what they cite, what they miss, and what that means for a brand that expects more shoppers to arrive through agents.

These docs open the hood on **ShoppiPanel**, our research rig for measuring that behavior. They are written for technical buyers, design partners, and operators who want to understand the method before they trust the report.

## What to read first

If you are new to the system, read [Open the Hood](/how-it-works/overview) first. It gives you the full map without forcing you through implementation detail.

If you are validating the methodology, start with [The Panel as an Instrument](/how-it-works/panel-as-an-instrument), then [Two Lanes](/how-it-works/two-lanes), then [The Honesty Layer](/evidence/honesty-layer).

If you are evaluating operational reliability, read [Anatomy of a Run](/evidence/anatomy-of-a-run) and [Run Lifecycle & Reliability](/evidence/run-lifecycle-and-reliability).

<Note>
  These pages describe the research method and evidence surface. They deliberately avoid packaging terms, internal operations, and private system details.
