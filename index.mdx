kya studies how AI agents shop: what they see, what they cite, and what they miss when they answer a shopper in your category.

These docs take you inside **ShoppiPanel**, our research rig for measuring that behavior — how a study is composed, how each agent shops, and how every number stays bound to the evidence behind it.

What you walk away with: a defensible read on how AI agents present your category to shoppers, every claim bound to a receipt you can open — so you act on it instead of guessing.
