The modern shopper is increasingly an AI agent arriving to your site.

kya labs is the research rig for measuring what those agents see, cite, and miss when they shop your category — and what that means for the brand on the other side of the answer.

Every study returns a report package: a one-line read, the structured data behind it, and a receipt for every number. Not a scraper dashboard with a mention count — a versioned instrument with the trips, the sources, and the access posture attached. A trend you can audit, not a score you have to trust.
