A delivered report is built so you can read it in five minutes or audit it line by line. Follow along in a live one: [a sample report](https://www.kyalabs.io/shoppipanel/sample).

Read it top to bottom — the one-line verdict, the count ledger behind it, the receipts the counts rest on, and the reach findings — and walk any number back to the trips that produced it.

## The reading order we recommend

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read the headline.">
    One line. The strategic "so what" sits with it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the count ledger.">
    Does the denominator support the verdict? The math is on the page, not behind it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a receipt.">
    Pick any number you'd quote in a meeting and walk it back to the trip. If the receipt holds, the number holds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the reach findings.">
    Where agents reached you, where they couldn't. Reach is reported per finding, as **reached** or **access-limited**, never as a flat refusal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Every report carries its own claim boundary on every surface — what the study measures and what it deliberately doesn't. See [What you get → What it is not](/insights/overview#what-it-is-not).
</Tip>
